About Me

Hi there! I'm Dirk, a double Master's student in Geo-Information Science and Climate Studies with a strong interest in Earth system science and the ways human activities are reshaping our planet. I have been fascinated by the Earth's processes, landscapes, and geography for as long as I can remember, making my academic path a natural progression of those interests. I began my studies with a Bachelor's degree in Environmental Sciences at Wageningen University & Research, where I am currently continuing my education through both Master's programmes. My academic interests currently centre on remote sensing and its applications within Earth system science, with a particular focus on air quality, land–atmosphere interactions, and environmental monitoring using geospatial data.
