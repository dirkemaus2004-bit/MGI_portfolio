About Me

Hi there! I'm Dirk, a Master's student in Geo-Information Science and Climate Studies at Wageningen University & Research, with a strong interest in understanding Earth system processes and how human activities influence the environment. My academic journey started with a Bachelor's degree in Environmental Sciences, where I developed a broad interest in climate systems, ecology, air quality, and the interactions between natural and human-driven processes on Earth. During my studies, I became increasingly interested in studying environmental change from a larger systems perspective: understanding patterns, trends, and connections across landscapes and ecosystems. This interest has shaped my current focus on remote sensing, spatial analysis, and the use of geospatial techniques to investigate environmental processes. Through my Master's programmes, I am developing skills in spatial analysis and remote sensing to better understand environmental processes and their changes over time.
